About Me

Yash Patil

Hello! I'm Yash Patil, a second-year BTech student at VIT Mumbai with a deep passion for photography, videography, and cinematography. For me, visual storytelling is more than just taking pictures — it's about capturing emotions, energy, and moments that people want to remember forever.

Photography started as a curiosity for me, but quickly turned into something much bigger. I love observing the small details that make a moment special — lighting, expressions, movement, and atmosphere. Whether it's a vibrant festival, a cinematic street moment, or a portrait that tells a story, I enjoy turning those moments into lasting memories.

Videography and cinematography allow me to push creativity even further. Through camera movement, framing, and editing, I enjoy creating visuals that feel dynamic and immersive. I love experimenting with cinematic techniques to make videos feel more powerful and engaging.

Outside of academics, I constantly explore new ways to improve my craft — experimenting with lighting, storytelling, editing styles, and creative compositions. Every shoot is an opportunity to learn something new and capture something unique.

My goal is to continue growing as a visual creator and build work that inspires people and preserves meaningful moments. Photography is not just about images for me — it’s about creating memories that last forever.
