About Me

Yash Patil

Hello! I'm Yash Patil, a second-year BTech student at VIT Mumbai with a deep passion for photography, videography, and cinematography. For me, visual storytelling goes beyond simply taking pictures — it's about capturing emotions, energy, and moments that deserve to be remembered forever.

Photography started as a curiosity but quickly became a passion. I love observing the small details that make a moment special — lighting, expressions, movement, and atmosphere. Whether it's a vibrant festival, a cinematic street scene, or a portrait that tells a story, I enjoy turning those moments into meaningful visual memories.

Videography and cinematography allow me to push creativity even further. Through camera movement, framing, and editing, I enjoy crafting visuals that feel immersive and dynamic. I love experimenting with cinematic techniques to create videos that feel powerful, emotional, and engaging.

Beyond academics, I constantly explore ways to grow as a visual creator — experimenting with lighting setups, storytelling techniques, editing styles, and creative compositions. Every shoot becomes an opportunity to learn something new and capture something unique.

My goal is to keep improving as a visual storyteller and build work that inspires people while preserving meaningful memories. For me, photography is not just about images — it's about creating moments that last forever.
